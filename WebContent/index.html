How it works

Three steps to smarter studying

From upload to discovery — SmartStudy keeps academic resources organized, searchable, and always within reach.

01

Upload your materials

Students and faculty upload PDFs, slides, and notes directly from any device. Files are indexed and made searchable instantly.

02

Organize by cohort

Admins assign resources to specific branches, semesters, and subjects. Every student sees exactly what's relevant to their course.

03

Access & collaborate

Browse, download, and build on shared resources. Peer notes and faculty materials live in one unified, always-synced library.

Features

Everything your campus needs

Built specifically for university workflows — not generic file storage.

📤
Upload

Drag-and-drop uploads

Share notes in seconds with bulk upload support. PDFs, presentations, and images are auto-converted for in-browser preview.

🔍
Search

Full-text search

Find any document by keyword, subject, or uploader. Search works inside PDFs and slide decks, not just file names.

🏷️
Organize

Smart categorization

Resources are automatically tagged by branch, year, and subject. Admins can override and create custom collections.

👥
Peers

Peer collaboration

Students can co-author notes, leave comments, and flag helpful resources. The best content rises to the top.

🔔
Alerts

Live sync notifications

Get notified the moment new resources are added to your cohort — no more hunting through email threads for attachments.

🛡️
Admin

Access control

Admins approve uploads, moderate content, and set visibility rules per department. Full audit trail included.
